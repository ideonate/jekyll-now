Our Process

Ideonate offers consultancy services by developer and entrepreneur Dan Lester.

  • Initial Advice

    Dan will take the time to discuss your project and ensure we are the best fit for you.
    Advice will always be available for free at this stage.

  • Engagement Plan

    As a serial entrepreneur, Dan will understand the commerical constraints of your business.
    He will find the simplest path to launch, seeing potential roadblocks early on and systematically eliminating them to keep on target.

  • Transition Plan

    As part of the project plan itself, we will agree a plan for continued support and operation of the finished product, or transition to your own team.

Ideonate can work on-site with companies in London or the Greater Cambridge area, and
may also undertake remote engagements.
