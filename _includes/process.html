Our Process

Ideonate offers consultancy services by developer and entrepreneur Dan Lester.

  • Initial Advice

    Dan will take the time to discuss your business and ensure we are the best fit for you.
    Advice will always be available for free at this stage.

  • Engagement Plan

    As a serial entrepreneur, Dan will understand the commerical constraints of your business.
    We will agree a plan for ad-hoc mentoring or a more formal advisory or fractional-CTO relationship.

  • Flexible Agreement

    We will review our relationship explicitly at least every three months. We will never require a commitment longer than a month's notice.

Ideonate can work on-site with companies in London or the Greater Cambridge area, and
may also undertake remote engagements.
