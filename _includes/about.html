About Dan

Dan Lester

Founder and Lead Developer

I have been a software developer and entrepreneur for over two decades, building and exiting a range of successful companies including VC-backed, bootstrapped, and agency businesses.

I was the technical co-founder of Conversocial, a VC-backed company acquired by Verint (Nasdaq: VRNT) for $50m in 2021.

Earlier exits include a Facebook agency business (iPlatform) sold to larger competitor Betapond; and WP-Glogin.com, a bootstrapped product business sold to a US-based portfolio company.

Since my last exit I have worked with a number of businesses in development and technical advisory roles, and also contributed to the open source ecosystem of data science tooling.
